Institute of Chemistry, Chinese Academy of Sciences

Prof. Su Weiping

Prof. Su Weiping is the group leader of the Su Group at the Institute of Chemistry, Chinese Academy of Sciences. His research focuses on dehydrogenative coupling, photocatalysis, and novel synthetic methods.

ICCAS LAB 302

Leading international collaborative research in transition-metal catalysis.

Pioneering Dehydrogenative Coupling & Photocatalysis
Our laboratory focuses on developing sustainable and efficient chemical methodologies. By harnessing the power of visible light and transition-metal catalysts, we aim to transform how complex molecules are constructed, reducing environmental impact while increasing molecular complexity.

Research Focus

Heterocyclic Synthesis

Innovative pathways for the construction of biologically relevant heterocyclic frameworks.

Visible Light Catalysis

Direct functionalization of inert C-H bonds to streamline molecular synthesis and minimize waste.

In-depth kinetic and computational studies to uncover the underlying logic of catalytic cycles.
